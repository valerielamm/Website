Explore Ecology

About: Explore Ecology provides youth ages 5 -13 with fun events that bring awareness of the environment in a welcoming manner. We do this by providing children with memorable experiences, teaching them relevant information, and making sure they stay entertained and stimulated in the process.

The Mission: The mission of Explore Ecology is to promote a greater understanding of the connections between people and their environment and to encourage creative thinking through hands-on environmental education and artistic expression. We empower our community to protect and preserve the environment through environmental education and creative exploration.

Creative Objective: Explore Ecology needs to unify the company’s branding across their wide range of events and activities provided. The design is going to achieve a clean and natural feel while also utilizing a broad range of colors, welcoming photography, and simple illustrations.

Logo Process

The goal was to combine two elements relevant to the company into one for the logo.

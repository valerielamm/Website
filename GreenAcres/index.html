Green Acres Golf & Games

About: Complex with mini-golf, go-karts, paintball, bumper boats, batting cages, party rooms and a snack bar.

Location: San Antonio, Texas.

Designed and Developed: New and improved website.

Click here to see live site.

Adaptable & Inviting

Icons: Emojis were used to liven up the text-heavy website in a fun manner. For consistency, small pngs were used instead of html entities. This ensures that the emojis are accessible and visually pleasing on all platforms.

Bootstrap: The structure of the webpage started from this template.

Imagery: More images were incorporated to be able to show what they have to offer.

Content: The goal was to make the large amount of info as organized, painless, and inviting as possible by using relevant visuals, strong typographic hierarchy, and a helpful menu to take the customer where they need to go to find information relevant to them.

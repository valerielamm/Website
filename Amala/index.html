Amala Foundation

About: The Amala Foundation unites people from all walks of life and inspires them to grow as individuals and as leaders in service to humanity.

The Problem: Participants and Guardians often comment on the long, repetitive registration process. Amala would like to address this issue as well as communication limitations within their community.

Project Brief: Make an app to solve the problem.

How Might We

Using the problem established, a set of How Might We questions were formed to encourage more design oppurtunities.

Focusing on What We Know

A class interview was held with Solomon Masala, who is in charge of Program oversight and is a Board Chair for the Amala Foundation.

Journey Mapping

Next, the app was mapped out to find efficient flows for the user and to visualize the app layout.

Visual Approach

An aspect that I love about Amala is how down to earth they are. Take one step into the building and you'll feel the love that this place and these people exert.

This is something I wanted my app to reflect.

 

The Amala Foundation was open to new colors and designs because a new website is on their agenda as well. Their logo was to remain the same.

I found that a clean way to depict the feel of their foundation was by using uneven, more natural edges throughout the app along with welcoming, natural colors.

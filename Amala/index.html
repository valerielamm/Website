Amala Foundation

About: The Amala Foundation unites people from all walks of life and inspires them to grow as individuals and as leaders in service to humanity.

The Problem: Participants and Guardians often comment on the long, repetitive registration process. Amala would like to address this issue as well as communication limitations within their community.

How Might We:
1. Encourage attendance to the programs.
2. Establish a community.
3. Make the sign-up process easier.
4. Keep our audience informed.

Journey Mapping:

Down to Earth

An aspect that I love about Amala is how down to earth they are. Take one step into the building and you'll feel the love that this place and these people exert.

This is something I wanted my app to reflect.

 

I found that a clean way to approach this was by using uneven, natural edges throughout the app.

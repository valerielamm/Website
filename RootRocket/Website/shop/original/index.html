Root Rocket™ a special mix of nutrients that have been horticulturalist tested and proven to help young plants in the transition from their nursery containers into the ground or a more permanent container.

The nutrients in each Root Rocket™ includes endo and ectomycorrhizae fungi, soluble sea kelp, soluble yucca plant extract, beneficial bacteria and Horta-Sorbe Water Management Gel.The fungi and beneficial bacteria go to work instantly, creating a nutritious environment for plants. The fungi brings old soil to life by filling it with nitrogen and phosphorous, for plant roots to enjoy.

If your plant is hungry it will enjoy the organic matter like the sea kelp and yucca plant extract because they contain a lot of minerals that will stimulate root growth.

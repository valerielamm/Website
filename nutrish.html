Nutrish

Showcased in AIGA Blue Ridge’s 2017 annual Flux Student Design Competition

Nutrish: Nutrish is an app designed to create a more nutritious, enjoyable, and less wasteful relationship users have with their food through the use of adaptive algorithms and devices.

Research Teammates: Jeff Davis, Stevie Boyrie, Christian Berg, Kristie Bocanegra, Nick Gatti, Christina Rodriguez, Rachael Briggs, Lorena Martinez, Austin Dennis

My Roles: Problem identification, persona building, user interface, google home

App Competitor Research

After analyzing 16 apps related to grocery shopping and nutrition tracking, these were the results.

Store

31.2%

Have features like coupons and sales to connect the users to nearby grocery stores.

Personal

81.5%

Allow users to be able to personalize their experience based on their activities.

List

43.7%

Allow users to manage a grocery shopping list and aid in shopping for items needed.

Organization

62.5%

Keeps users organized through calendar features, meal plans and saved recipes.

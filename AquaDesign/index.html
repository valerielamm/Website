Aqua Design

Logo Award: Silver Award: Typography4

Assignment: This is student work for a theoretical client. The logo needed to reflect the company's mission, which is to embrace innovation.

Approach: A fish was strategically incorporated in a way to suggest that it's swimming behind the two main letterforms, towards brightness. The tail becomes a replacement for the crossbar.

Results: Having the letter 'A' and 'D' touching each other and having the fish become a part of them resulted in the logo becoming a solid mark.

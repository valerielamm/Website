Nutrish: Nutrish is an app designed to create a more nutritious, enjoyable, and less wasteful relationship users have with their food through the use of adaptive algorithms and devices.

The Problem: Due to lack of time, people struggle with taking nutrition into account and scheduling family dinners, which has been shown to negatively affect our health and happiness. This also inadvertently contributes to the growing problem of food waste.

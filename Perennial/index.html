Perennial

About: Perennial is a theoretical app thats goal is to allow one to manage, share, get inspired by, and learn about gardening.

Creative Objective: The app should be clean, user friendly, and take advantage of nature's beauty through the use of imagery. Here's my inspiration board.

Naming: The chosen name is 'Perennial'. Perennials are plants that edure well and grow for a long time. The name is strong, simple, positive, and relevant.

Logo: When developing the logo, the goal was to keep the shape simple by using simplistic vector forms. Floral aspects were considered during development.
